Support us

Boulder County Gender Support

Boulder's group meets from 7-9pm every Thursday @ Out Boulder's Pridehouse (2132 14th St, Boulder 80302).

Longmont's group meets from 6:30-8:30 pm every Tuesday @ Out Boulder County on Main (463 Main St. Longmont, CO 80501).

Out! Community Cafe

Out! Community Cafe is a dynamic, inclusive space that encourages, inspires and validates everyone, as we share who we are and where we’re at in our lives.

We will meet in Longmont (463 Main St) on 1st and 3rd Wednesdays from 4 to 5:30 PM and in Boulder (2132 14th St) on 2nd and 4th Wednesdays from 5 to 6:30 PM.

Longmont Youth Group

This group brings in community speakers, and has small group activities, social events, art activities, and so much more. Allies and welcome and encouraged to join this space!

When: 2nd & 4th Thursdays of the month from 4:00 - 6:00pm 
Location: Longmont YMCA - 950 Lashley St. Longmont, CO 80504

Become a Monthly Donor

Please join our Board of Directors in authorizing monthly contributions of $25, $50 or $100 to give us the security and the flexibility we need to continue providing services, programs and support.

Everything we do requires staff whose dedication is unparalleled. Donate Today.

LGBTQ Youth Group

Join us for Creative Writing & Performance on Tuesdays, 4:30 - 6 pm. This program is designed for youth of all skill levels who are interested in art and LGBTQ issues. 

We write poems, make jewelry, do tie-dye, color, paint, draw, and much more. 

Gay Men's Knitting Group - Alternate Saturdays

All knitting levels are welcome. A teacher will be on hand with yarn and needles to help you get started. Just bring $5 to cover the cost of supplies. If you're an experienced knitter bring your project and join us for an afternoon of good company and the magic of knitting!
